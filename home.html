FUTURE IMPOSTER

Concepto Hipnotico - Mephyst - Spectralband


'Future Imposter' is alias created by Montenegrin producer and dj 'Velibor Miranovic'. Sound of 'Future Imposter' is pure techno. Hypnotic, dark, raw and thrilling sounds. His first release under this alias was released for 'Mephyst', a lebel owned by 'Modular Phaze'. This release had a support from almost all major techno acts. Music from this producer was already supported by Richie Hawtin, Paula Cazeneve, Forest People, Vegim, Vertical Spectrum, Modular Phaze, Bruno Ledesma and many more. After 'Mephyst' a new tracks appeared on well known 'Concepto Hipnotico' from Buenos Aires, and had a great success. A label owner 'Bruno Ledesma' personaly selected a track 'Future Imposter - Blok 5 (Original Mix)' to be in 'Best of 2016' compilaiton. Next release is on Spectralband. Digital EP and V.A. Vinyl. Two original tracks and remix. In just a year 'Future Imposter' became a well know act on a national and regional scale. Every dancefloor where he played was rocked. This man as DJ will show you a great energy, so much love and passion, and will make you decide to dance.
